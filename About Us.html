About Us

What we do

Introduction

Infiniti Marine Pte Ltd was incorporated in Yr 2006 with a strength of 3 staff operating in Jurong Port. We have since grown and currently, we have 2 offices in Singapore and 1 office in Batam with a combined staff strength of 26 to oversee our expanding operations.

Company Mission and Quality Statement

The Management of Infiniti Marine Pte Ltd has endorsed a culture of continual improvement in its journey towards business excellence. To achieve excellence, the Management has set the following mission statement and quality policy:

To Be A Leader In The Oil And Gas Support Logistics Industry By Providing High Quality Equipment And Excellent Service.

To Be Efficient In Our Execution To Meet Our Customers Expectation. We shall at all times be aware of quality considerations in our work. This ensures the output of our work meets the quality expectations of our customers.

We will strive to meet this initiative by continually improving on our committed objectives in relation to the above mission statement so as to maintain high customer satisfaction and lasting business relationship.

Infiniti Marine Pte Ltd is currently BizSAFE Star, ISO 9001:2008, OHSAS 18001 certified.
